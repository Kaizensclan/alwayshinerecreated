Always Shine

Always Shine is a Clear, Liquid, Nano Ceramic Coating, which chemically bonds with any surface, forming a Rigid, Nano Glass super – structure protecting the surface. The shielding is permanent, resistant to Solvents, acids, alkalis, UV rays, harsh weather conditions, fire and corrosion. This preserves the original factory paint, makes the surface glossy, Self Cleaning and super hydrophobic.

Best Ceramic Coating and PPF Films in India

Always Shine is the global leader in nanoceramic surface protection, offering the best ceramic coating and PPF films in India . We provide a range of ceramic coating and paint protection film products, each formulated for specific surfaces. Our ceramic coatings and PPF are designed for automotive, marine, aviation, and industrial applications. The coating formulas are molecularly engineered for surfaces such as paint, vinyl, polymers, glass, and more. Always Shine coatings bond at a molecular level, filling nano-pores and creating a hydrophobic surface that resists contamination. Naturally, our KAVACA PPF comes pre-coated with our flagship ceramic coating. This makes our products the top choice for critical applications across all industries.
